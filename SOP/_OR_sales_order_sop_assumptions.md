# Oregon Market Sales Order Lifecycle SOP Pack

**Portfolio use case:** Sales Data Coordinator / Sales Operations support
**Market:** Oregon cannabis wholesale / retail account operations
**Systems assumed:** CRM, ERP, Metrc / Oregon Cannabis Tracking System, inventory/WMS, shared documentation drive
**Version:** v0.1 draft
**Owner:** Sales Operations / Sales Data Coordinator

---

## Purpose

This SOP pack documents how a Sales Data Coordinator can support the end-to-end sales order lifecycle in a regulated Oregon cannabis market. The goal is to show control over order accuracy, system data integrity, compliance checkpoints, fulfillment coordination, invoicing support, credit memo workflows, and cross-functional issue resolution.

These SOPs are written for a simulated portfolio project and should be adapted to the employer’s actual ERP, CRM, warehouse, accounting, and compliance systems.

---

## Oregon Market Operating Assumptions

1. Orders are business-to-business sales to licensed Oregon cannabis retailers or other licensed entities.
2. Cannabis product movement must be traceable through Metrc / Oregon CTS using package UIDs and transfer manifests.
3. Fulfillment cannot proceed until customer account, license, product, quantity, inventory, pricing, and delivery details are validated.
4. Sales orders, fulfillment records, Metrc manifests, invoices, and credit memos must reconcile across systems.
5. Rejections, refusals, returns, substitutions, shortages, and delivery changes require documentation and cross-functional approval.
6. Order exceptions must be logged with owner, root cause, status, resolution, and final system correction.

---

## Core Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Sales Data Coordinator | Owns sales order accuracy, edits, system updates, documentation, discrepancy follow-up, and daily QC checks |
| Sales Representative | Submits customer order request, confirms customer intent, handles relationship communication |
| Inventory / Warehouse | Confirms available inventory, pick status, substitutions, shortages, and fulfillment readiness |
| Distribution / Driver Team | Coordinates delivery route, transfer timing, manifest support, and delivery confirmation |
| Compliance | Advises on state-specific requirements, license issues, Metrc exceptions, and regulated corrections |
| Accounting / AR | Posts invoices, processes credit memos, reconciles billing discrepancies |
| Customer / Retailer | Receives order, accepts or rejects product, confirms delivery documents and invoice questions |
